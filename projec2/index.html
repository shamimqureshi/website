The City

Hydrabad is the Capital of Southern India's Telagana state.A major center of the tecnology industry,it's home to many upscale restaurant and shops. Its Historic sites include Golconda fort, a former shops diamond trading center that was Qutb shahi dynastic capital.The charminar, a 16th-centuary mosque whose 4 arches support towering minarets,is an old city landmark near the long standing Laad Bazaar.
